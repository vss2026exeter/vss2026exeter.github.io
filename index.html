Scope

The scope of the workshop is on the theoretical developments and practical applications using the concept of variable structure systems, sliding mode control, and observation.

Specific interests are:

On the theoretical front: First and Higher Order Sliding Mode, Suboptimal Sliding Mode, Chattering analysis, Discrete-time realisations, Lyapunov Methods for VSS & SMOC/O, Homogeneity Methods for VSS & SMOC/C, Adaptive Sliding Mode, Learning and Data-based Sliding Mode, Applications of VSS ideas to other theoretical problems

and on the applications end
